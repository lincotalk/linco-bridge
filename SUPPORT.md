# Support

## Community Support

- Use GitHub Issues for reproducible bugs and clear feature requests.
- Use the community channels for general questions and usage discussion.
- Use the private security channel for vulnerabilities.
- Include version, OS, installation method, agent type, reproduction steps, and redacted logs.

## What Maintainers Can Usually Help With

- Repository documentation gaps, onboarding problems, and broken links.
- Reproducible bugs in the open-source connector or reference platform.
- Protocol compatibility questions related to documented public behavior.
- Clarification of supported evaluation paths, demo flows, and integration boundaries.

## What Is Usually Out of Scope

- Internal company environments, unpublished products, or private deployment assets.
- Debugging custom forks without a minimal reproduction on the public repository.
- Immediate support for every third-party integration or every historical version.
- Reverse engineering behavior from screenshots alone when logs, steps, or version info are missing.

## How Issues May Be Triaged

- Missing reproduction details may be routed back for clarification.
- General questions may be redirected to community channels.
- Issues outside the published support boundary may be closed with explanation.

Community support is best effort. The project does not guarantee fixed availability, response time, resolution time, compensation, or permanent compatibility with third-party tools.

Enterprise deployment, custom adapters, integration consulting, and contractual support are outside the community support scope.

# 支持边界

## 社区支持

- 可稳定复现的缺陷和清晰的功能请求，请使用 GitHub Issues。
- 一般问题和使用交流请使用社区入口。
- 安全漏洞请使用私密安全反馈渠道。
- 请提供版本、系统、安装方式、Agent 类型、复现步骤和脱敏日志。

## 维护者通常会处理的内容

- 仓库文档缺口、上手问题和失效链接。
- 开源连接器或参考平台中可稳定复现的缺陷。
- 与公开文档行为相关的协议兼容问题。
- 支持的体验路径、Demo 流程和集成边界说明。

## 通常不在支持范围内

- 公司内部环境、未公开产品或私有部署资源。
- 没有基于公开仓库提供最小复现的自定义 fork 调试。
- 对所有第三方集成或所有历史版本提供即时支持。
- 只有截图、缺少日志、步骤或版本信息时，要求反推出完整行为。

## 问题通常如何分流

- 缺少复现信息的问题可能会被要求补充说明。
- 一般问题可能会被引导到社区渠道。
- 超出公开支持边界的问题可能会在说明原因后关闭。

社区支持尽力而为。项目不承诺固定可用率、响应时间、修复时间、赔偿或对第三方工具的永久兼容。

企业部署、定制 Adapter、集成咨询和合同支持不属于社区支持范围。
