# Code of Conduct

We want Linco Bridge to be a respectful, technically honest, and welcoming project.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas and code without attacking people.
- Respect different experience levels, languages, and backgrounds.
- Provide reproducible evidence and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Protect credentials, personal data, and unpublished vulnerabilities.
- Accept maintainer decisions even when a proposal is not included.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or targeted insults.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Spam, misleading promotion, undisclosed paid recommendations, or repeated disruption.
- Deliberately submitting malicious code, dependencies, or security bypasses.

## Enforcement Approach

- Maintainers may request edits, hide content, lock threads, close discussions, reject contributions, or restrict participation depending on severity.
- Repeated or severe misconduct may result in temporary or permanent removal from repository interaction spaces.
- Enforcement decisions are made to protect contributors, users, and the safety of the project.

## Reporting Guidance

- When reporting a conduct issue, include links, screenshots, timestamps, and a short factual summary when available.
- Do not repost private or harmful content more widely than needed for moderation.
- If a report also contains a security problem, use the security process in `SECURITY.md` for the technical details.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers at `369805210@qq.com` or through platform moderation tools. Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or ban participation when needed to protect the community.

# 社区行为规范

我们希望 Linco Bridge 是一个尊重他人、技术表述诚实且对新参与者友好的项目。

## 期望行为

- 讨论想法和代码时，不攻击个人。
- 尊重不同经验水平、语言和背景。
- 提供可复现证据，并披露利益冲突。
- 保护凭证、个人数据和未公开漏洞。
- 即使某个提案没有被采纳，也尊重维护者决定。

## 不可接受的行为

- 骚扰、歧视、威胁或定向侮辱。
- 发布私人信息或凭证。
- 垃圾信息、误导性推广、未披露的付费推荐或重复扰乱。
- 故意提交恶意代码、恶意依赖或安全绕过。

## 处理方式

- 维护者可根据严重程度要求修改内容、隐藏内容、锁定讨论、关闭讨论、拒绝贡献或限制参与。
- 重复或严重的不当行为可能导致临时或永久移出仓库互动空间。
- 处理决定以保护贡献者、用户和项目安全为目标。

## 举报说明

- 举报行为问题时，请尽量提供链接、截图、时间点和简短事实摘要。
- 不要在不必要范围内继续传播私密或有害内容。
- 如果举报中同时包含安全问题，请按 `SECURITY.md` 的安全流程提交技术细节。

行为问题请通过 `369805210@qq.com` 或平台治理工具私密联系维护者。为保护社区，维护者可在必要时编辑、隐藏、拒绝内容或限制参与。
